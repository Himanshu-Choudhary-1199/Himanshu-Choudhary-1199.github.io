About

I'm Himanshu Choudhary a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Mathematical & Statistical Sciences at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi, India, working with Prof. Manoj Thakur.

My research interests primarily focus on developing Machine Learning and Soft Computing techniques, with a particular emphasis on their applications in Finance and Portfolio Optimization. This includes exploring advanced computational methods, reinforcement learning, and AI-driven financial models to enhance decision-making, optimize asset allocation, and improve risk management strategies in dynamic financial environments.
